# Certification ROI: Knowledge

Certs are a tiebreaker, not a substitute for a project. Cloud/platform certs tend to
show the biggest salary signal, but **after 2–3 solid certs the ROI collapses, shift
to projects and depth.** Recommend by demand × effort × the owner's trajectory.

## How to rank certs for THIS owner
1. **Demand:** count the cert (or its platform) in the owner's analyzed JDs and
   `trends.certFrequency`. A cert nobody in their market asks for scores zero,
   whatever its global prestige.
2. **Adjacency:** a cert that formalizes a strong-tier skill is cheap signal; a cert
   requiring a missing foundation is really two recommendations (flag it as such).
3. **Effort vs window:** prefer ≤ 4–6 week preparations while the owner is actively
   applying; long certifications only between pipelines or for career pivots.
4. **Market specials:** national programs/bootcamps with placement (see
   `market_playbook.md`) often beat vendor certs for early-career owners, check there
   first.

## Strategy rules
- **Max ~3 certs total** in a recommendation horizon; sequence cheapest-fastest first
  to build momentum.
- A cert only counts on the CV when paired with a project using the same skill, 
  always bundle the project into the same careerAdvice item.
- Re-validate rankings quarterly, cert markets shift fast.

## Output shape
Each `careerAdvice` item: the cert, why now (tie to a real JD trend the owner has
seen), effort, expected impact, and 2–3 concrete action items (course/registration
link, the companion project). Surface the top 1–2, never a list of ten.
